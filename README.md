# Editable Resume App - User Guide

## Overview

The Editable Resume App is a professional resume builder that allows you to create, edit, and export your resume in a clean, modern format. You can switch between preview and edit modes, customize all sections, and print or save your resume as a PDF.

## Powered by Manus

This application is built with React 19, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS, providing a responsive and modern user interface. The app runs entirely in your browser with local storage persistence, ensuring your resume data is saved automatically as you make changes.

## Using Your Website

### Viewing Your Resume

When you first open the application, you'll see your resume displayed in a professional format. The resume includes your contact information, professional summary, projects, education, languages, and certificates. All sections are clearly organized with proper spacing and typography for a polished appearance.

### Editing Your Resume

Click the **Edit** button in the top navigation bar to switch to edit mode. In edit mode, you can modify any field in your resume:

- **Header Section**: Update your full name and job title
- **Contact Information**: Edit your email, website, phone number, and location
- **Professional Summary**: Modify your professional summary text
- **Projects**: Add new projects using the "Add Project" button, or edit existing ones by changing the title, date, and description
- **Education**: Add education entries with institution name, date, degree, and major
- **Languages**: Add languages and proficiency levels
- **Certificates**: Add certificates with title, date, and organization

All changes are automatically saved to your browser's local storage.

### Adding and Removing Sections

For projects, education, and certificates, you can add new entries by clicking the "Add [Section]" button. Each new entry will appear as an editable card. To remove an entry, click the trash icon on the right side of the card.

### Previewing Your Changes

Click the **Preview** button (which appears when in edit mode) to switch back to the resume view and see how your changes look.

### Printing or Saving as PDF

Click the **Print/PDF** button to open your browser's print dialog. From there, you can:
- Print your resume directly to a physical printer
- Save your resume as a PDF file by selecting "Save as PDF" from the print options

The resume is formatted to look professional when printed, with proper page breaks and styling.

### Resetting to Default

Click the **Reset** button to restore the resume to its original content. You'll be asked to confirm before the reset happens.
